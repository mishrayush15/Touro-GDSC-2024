Drugs and related laws

Alcohol and Smoking Regulations:

- Legal Drinking Age - The legal drinking age in Japan is 20.

- Drinking in Public - Drinking in public places is generally acceptable, but public intoxication is discouraged.

- Smoking Areas - Smoking in public places is regulated. Designated smoking areas are available, and smoking in non- designated areas may result in fines.

- Age Verification - Retailers may request age verification when purchasing alcohol or tobacco



Drug Laws:

- Strict Penalties - Japan has strict laws against the possession, use, and trafficking of illegal drugs. Penalties for drug-related offenses are severe, including lengthy prison sentences.

- Prescription Medication - Some prescription medications that are legal in other countries may be restricted or prohibited in Japan. It's advisable to check with the Japanese embassy or consulate before traveling.
